## Business Rules
- **Face-to-Face (F2F) is required starting from the 3rd benefit period onward**
- **Valid F2F window**: 0 to 30 days before the Benefit Period Start Date (BPSD)
- **Days calculation**: `Days = (F2F Completed Date) - (BP Start Date)` (includes F2F date, excludes BPSD)
- **Claim-to-BP mapping**: `benefitPeriodStartDate <= claimEndDate <= benefitPeriodEndDate`
- **F2F visit disciplines**: MD or NP only
- **DB table**: `claims_face_to_face_visits` (billing_claims DB)

## Error Messages
1. **No F2F exists**: `Patient is missing Face to Face visit (there are no F2F visits recorded in the System)`
2. **F2F outside valid window**: `Patient is missing Face to Face visit ({days} days between Benefit Period Start Date: {BPSD} and F2F Completed Date: {f2fDate})`

## Preconditions
- Hospice patient admitted with Medicare benefits, backdated >6 months to reach 3rd benefit period
- Written certifications added for all benefit periods (BP1, BP2, BP3)
- Login as MD role

## F2F Visit Recording Flow (Care Plan)
1. Navigate to Care Plan from black nav bar
2. Click **+** to add a new visit
3. Select **Role** dropdown = "Medical Director", **Type** = "Face to Face Visit"
4. Click **Submit** (handle white screen: refresh, navigate back to patient > Care Plan)
5. Click **Record Visit** in the grid
6. Fill **Vitals** section (Blood Pressure is sufficient)
7. Fill **Face to Face** section: narrative, attestation checkbox, name of signatory (copy from vitals error popup), date
8. Click **Complete** button
9. On Complete popup: enter valid start/end date and time, click **Submit**
